Beyond the code

My Hobbies & Interests

I love coding — diving into a hard problem, sketching a solution, and watching it come together line by line. Side projects and late-night refactors are where I do my best thinking.

New frameworks, new languages, new ways of thinking about old problems — I'm rarely without a course, a book, or a half-finished tutorial open in another tab.

A good frame is just another kind of problem-solving — light, timing, composition. I like wandering with a camera and seeing what the city or the landscape decides to show me.

New places reset how I think. Every trip ends up feeding back into work somehow — a different pace, a different perspective on what actually matters.
